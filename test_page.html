Assignment 4: maps


Pointwise concentration of trees - dot density map

The following dot density maps show where trees are concentrated in the USA at a city/pointwise level. You can switch between two alternative views thanks to the radio button to include details on the specific tree species. You can drag the map in order to navigate it or zoom in/out by clicking on a state/by scrolling.
Moreover, the legend of the second visualization is interactive: you can click on one or more tree species to reduce the visualisation only to those ones.
Hover on states or circles in order to get more information about them.





Aggregated concentration of trees - choropleth map

The following choropleth maps show where trees are concentrated in the USA at a state/aggregated level. You can switch between two alternative views thanks to the radio button to include details on the tree density for each state, computed as the ratio between tree abundance and state area.
Moreover, you find a legend on the right side of both maps. Grey-striped areas fill states for which we have no information about trees.
Hover on states in order to get more information about them.
